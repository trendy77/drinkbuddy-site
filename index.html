FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you download.

Is Drink Buddy free?

Yes. The free version is ad-supported. A paid version is also available which removes ads entirely.

Does it need an account?

No. Drink Buddy requires zero sign-up. All your data is stored locally on your device — nothing is shared or uploaded.

How accurate is the BAC calculator?

Drink Buddy uses the Widmark formula, which is the scientifically validated method used in medical research and legal blood alcohol estimation. Results are estimates — individual metabolism varies.

Is this app for Android?

Yes, Drink Buddy is currently available on Android via Google Play.

What's the difference between the free and paid versions?

The free version covers everyday tracking — logging drinks, BAC estimates and your recent history. Premium removes all in-app advertisements and unlocks extra features: graphs and analytics, unlimited drink history (free shows the last 30 days), export and import of your drinking data, and custom drinks.

Is this app suitable for minors?

No. Drink Buddy is intended for adults aged 18 and over. It is a drink tracking tool — not a product that promotes alcohol consumption.
